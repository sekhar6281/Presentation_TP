IR Detection
System

940nm infrared LEDs flood the theatre screen — invisible to the human eye, but completely overexpose any recording camera.

Arrays of 940nm IR LEDs mounted around the screen emit a continuous invisible flood of infrared light across the entire display area.

940nm is beyond the visible spectrum. The audience sees nothing — the movie plays perfectly as intended with no disturbance.
03

Phone cameras detect IR wavelengths. The sensor gets completely flooded — recording turns to pure white glare, totally unusable.

TPVM
Technology

Temporal Pixel Visual Masking adds 7 invisible layers to every movie frame — perfect for the audience, completely distorted for any recording camera.

01
7 Invisible Layers Per Frame
TPVM injects 7 imperceptible masking layers onto every frame of the movie in real time during projection.
02
Audience Sees Perfectly
Human persistence of vision merges the layers into a single clear image — the theatre experience is completely unaffected.
03
Recording Becomes Unusable
Camera sensors capture each layer separately, producing a distorted, flickering, unwatchable recording.
